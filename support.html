Frequently Asked Questions

Is Eat Cleanly free to use?

Yes! You can browse all recipes, view macro breakdowns, and explore the app for free. Premium features like weekly meal planning, unlimited saves, and advanced filters are available with a subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Eat Cleanly, and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app, go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Can I request new recipes?

Absolutely! We're always adding new content. Send your recipe requests or dietary needs to support@eatcleanly.app and we'll do our best to include them.

Do you support dietary restrictions?

Yes. You can set allergen filters (gluten, dairy, nuts, soy, etc.) and dietary preferences in Settings → Content to personalize your recipe feed.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account within the app. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. Please note this action cannot be undone.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Check your internet connection for recipe loading issues
  • Restart your device if the app becomes unresponsive
  • If issues persist, email us with your device model and iOS version
